Lesis (TopoL) to SQLite
=======================

Conversion of the Lesis database (TopoL-L GIS) to SQLite format to open in NextGIS QGIS.

Launch tool: https://toolbox.nextgis.com/operation/lesis2sqlite

**Try it out using our sample:**

Download `input dataset <https://nextgis.ru/data/toolbox/lesis2sqlite/lesis2sqlite_inputs.zip>`_ to test the instrument. Step-by-step instructions included.

Get the `output <https://nextgis.ru/data/toolbox/lesis2sqlite/lesis2sqlite_outputs.zip>`_ to additionally check the results.
